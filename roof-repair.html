Find the leak, not just the symptom

Water is sneaky. It can enter the roof in one spot and show up on a ceiling ten feet away, so the stain almost never sits directly under the failure. The biggest mistake we see is a quick patch over a wet spot that does nothing about the actual entry point — which means the leak comes right back the next time it rains. We trace water back to its source, then fix the cause.

And we'll be straight with you. If your roof is near the end of its life and a repair would just be money down the drain, we'll say so and show you why — no scare tactics, no pressure. When a repair genuinely makes sense, that's exactly what we'll recommend.

What we repair

Leaks & water intrusion

Active drips, ceiling stains, and attic moisture traced back to the true source and sealed for good — before they turn into rot or mold.

Missing & damaged shingles

Wind-lifted, cracked, curling, or blown-off shingles replaced with a proper color and profile match so the repair blends in.

Flashing & penetrations

Failed flashing around chimneys, skylights, valleys, and vent pipes — the most common leak points — re-sealed and rebuilt correctly.

Repair, or replace?

Sometimes a leak is a sign the whole roof is done. If your roof is past 20 years, losing granules into the gutters, or failing in several places at once, a repair can be throwing good money after bad. We'll give you the honest math — and if a full roof replacement is the better value, we'll lay out exactly why. If the damage came from a storm, your insurance may cover it — and our owner is a licensed public adjuster who can manage that claim for you.
