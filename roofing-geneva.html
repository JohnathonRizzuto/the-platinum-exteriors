Geneva roofs are not one-size-fits-all

Drive through Geneva and the housing tells a story: stately older homes near the downtown and the river, steep-pitched Victorians with intricate rooflines, and newer construction filling in on the west side. A roof that suits one rarely suits the next. The wrong shingle color or profile on a historic home looks off the moment it's done — and on a complex roofline, sloppy flashing and valley work is exactly where leaks start.

We're a local, Batavia-based team — not a national franchise running a one-style playbook. We match the materials, the color, and the detail work to the home in front of us, and we give a straight assessment of what your roof actually needs.
