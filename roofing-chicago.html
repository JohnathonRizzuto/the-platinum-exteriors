Chicagoland storm season, handled

Suburban Chicago sees its share of severe weather every year — spring and summer bring hail and straight-line winds rolling in from the west, and that's exactly the kind of storm that quietly wrecks a roof. Most of the damage doesn't announce itself; it shows up later as a leak, a stain, or a failed warranty claim.

Our owner is a licensed public adjuster, which sets us apart from a typical roofer. We inspect and document the storm damage, file your insurance claim, and manage it through to payout — working for you, not the insurance company. You get a new roof and an advocate in one.
