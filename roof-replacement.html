When it's time to replace, not repair

A roof rarely fails all at once — it warns you first. If your roof is past 20 years, shedding granules into the gutters, showing widespread curling or missing shingles, or leaking in more than one spot, patching it usually just delays the inevitable. A full replacement resets the clock, restores your home's protection, and often pays for itself in energy savings and resale value.

We give you a straight assessment — if a repair will genuinely hold, we'll tell you. We only recommend a replacement when it's the right call for your home and your wallet.

Shingle systems we install

Architectural

Layered, dimensional shingles with real depth — heavier, more durable, and rated 30–50 years with high wind ratings. The most popular choice for lasting curb appeal.

3-Tab

Clean, uniform, single-layer shingles at a budget-friendly price point — a solid option when cost is the priority and the structure suits it.

Metal & Standing Seam

40+ years of performance with superior wind, fire, and snow resistance and energy-saving reflectivity — often the last roof you'll ever buy.

What a Platinum replacement includes

  • Complete tear-off and disposal of the old roof — no shingling over problems.
  • Full inspection and repair of the decking before anything new goes down.
  • Proper underlayment, ice-and-water shield, and corrected attic ventilation.
  • Manufacturer-spec installation that protects your warranty coverage.
  • A thorough magnetic nail sweep and full cleanup — we leave it better than we found it.
