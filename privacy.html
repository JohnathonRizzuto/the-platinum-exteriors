Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Platinum Construction Group Inc ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use our website or contact us. We keep this simple and only collect what we need to serve you.

Information we collect

When you fill out a quote request or contact form on our site, we collect the information you choose to give us, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (optional)
  • The type of work you need and any project details you share

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your request and provide a free estimate
  • Contact you by phone, text, or email about the work you asked about
  • Schedule and complete services you request

By submitting a form, you agree to be contacted by phone, text, or email regarding your inquiry. Message and data rates may apply, and you can ask us to stop contacting you at any time.

How your information is handled

Form submissions on our website are delivered to us through a trusted third-party form-delivery service that sends your message to our business email. Information is transmitted over a secure (HTTPS) connection.

We do not sell your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We only share it with service providers who help us operate (for example, our form-delivery service), and only as needed to respond to you, or where required by law.

Cookies & analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. This information is general and is not used to personally identify you. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Your choices

You may request that we update or delete the information you've shared with us, or ask us to stop contacting you, by reaching out using the contact details below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
