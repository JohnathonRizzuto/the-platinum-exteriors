More than a facelift

Siding does two jobs at once: it sets the entire look of your home, and it keeps water and weather out of the structure behind it. When siding cracks, warps, fades, or pulls away from the wall, both jobs start to fail — and moisture getting in behind old or damaged siding is how small problems turn into rot, mold, and expensive structural repairs.

A proper siding replacement fixes the curb appeal and the protection in one move. New siding instantly modernizes the home, can improve energy efficiency, and is one of the highest-return exterior upgrades you can make — while giving you peace of mind that the walls underneath are sealed and sound.

Siding options we install

Vinyl

The most popular choice — low maintenance, durable, and available in a huge range of colors and profiles. A strong value that holds its look for years with little upkeep.

Fiber Cement

Premium, heavy-duty siding that resists fire, rot, and insects and carries a high-end wood-grain look. An outstanding mix of curb appeal and long-term durability.

Trim, Soffit & Fascia

The finishing details that complete the look and seal the edges — we replace worn trim, soffit, and fascia so the whole exterior is tight and consistent.

What a Platinum siding job includes

  • Careful removal of the old siding and inspection of the wall behind it.
  • Repair of any sheathing or framing damage we find before new siding goes up.
  • Proper house wrap and moisture barrier — the step that keeps water out for good.
  • Manufacturer-spec installation with clean, consistent lines and tight detailing.
  • A complete cleanup and a final walkthrough — we leave the property spotless.
