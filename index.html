What We Do

Complete exterior solutions, under one roof.

Six exterior services, one accountable team. Whether you need a quick repair or a full restoration after a storm, Platinum does it right the first time.

Roofing

Tear-offs, repairs, and full replacements in architectural, 3-tab, and metal — installed to GAF spec to stand up to Illinois wind, hail, and snow.

Siding

Vinyl, fiber-cement, and premium lap siding that transforms curb appeal and seals your home tight against the elements.

Gutter Systems

Seamless gutters and leaf guards engineered to move water away from your foundation — and keep it there, season after season.

Solar Solutions

Roof-integrated solar with financing built in. Lower energy bills, higher home value, and zero guesswork from quote to switch-on.

Painting & Coating

Exterior painting and protective coatings that refresh the look and seal every surface — adding years of life to your home.

Windows & Doors

Energy-efficient windows and doors that cut drafts, cut bills, and finish the look of a freshly restored exterior.
