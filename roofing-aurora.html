One roofer for a city full of different roofs

Aurora's housing stock spans more than a hundred years. The older homes along the river and in the historic districts often have steep pitches, multiple dormers, and slate or wood that's been covered over a few times. The post-war neighborhoods have the classic ranches and split-levels. And the newer subdivisions out toward Eola and the east and west edges of town are full of two-stories with complex roof lines. Each of those needs a different eye — and that's exactly what we bring.

We're based in Batavia, a few minutes up the Fox River from Aurora, so a real local crew handles your roof from the first inspection to the final cleanup. We're Owens Corning Preferred and GAF Certified, which means we install to manufacturer spec and your warranty actually holds up.

After the storm, we handle the claim

The same storms that roll across the Fox Valley hit Aurora hard, and an older roof takes a beating from hail and high wind. A lot of that damage hides until the next leak shows up inside — by which point a small problem has become an expensive one.

Our owner is a licensed public adjuster, so when your roof is storm-damaged we do more than re-shingle it.
